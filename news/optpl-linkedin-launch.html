XXVII ONPCE Brings Together 100+ Researchers at UFSCar Sorocaba

Date: March 13, 2026 Author: OptPL Communications Location: UFSCar, Sorocaba, SP

OptPL on Social Media

The workshop also marked the public launch of the OptPL brand on social media. The thematic project is now active on LinkedIn under the name OptPL — Optimization in Production and Logistics, where it will share research updates, interviews with team members, practical applications, and news from the project. Participants were invited to follow and engage with the page to stay connected between workshops.

The next edition, XXVIII ONPCE, is expected to be held in 2027 at a location to be announced. The organizing committee thanked FAPESP for the continued support that makes the workshop series possible.
